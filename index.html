Download ARIA Installer for Mac

macOS only. Requires Apple Silicon (M1 or later). ~8GB download.

How it works
  1. Download and open the installer file above
  2. Let it run for 10-15 minutes (it downloads AI models)
  3. Connect your accounts in the setup wizard that opens automatically
  4. Get your first briefing tomorrow morning
What you'll need
  • A Mac with Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3, or M4)
  • At least 15GB of free disk space
  • Your Gmail account credentials
  • A Telegram account (free app, any phone)
  • About 15 minutes
Your data stays on your computer

ARIA runs entirely on your Mac. Your emails, calendar, and patient data are never uploaded to the cloud. The AI models run locally on your hardware.
